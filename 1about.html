About Filipino Sign Language

Discover the beauty and importance of Filipino Sign Language (FSL) and how our platform helps in learning and communication.

What is Filipino Sign Language?

Filipino Sign Language (FSL) is the national sign language of the Philippines, recognized as the official mode of communication for the Filipino Deaf community.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the communication gap by providing an accessible and interactive way to learn FSL through technology.

Why Learn FSL?

Learning FSL promotes inclusivity, helps communicate with the Deaf community, and preserves an important part of Filipino heritage and culture.
